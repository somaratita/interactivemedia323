How    really    sad    it    must    be

     To      be      the      last      of      a      dying      breed


       Nobody        left        but        me.



         The          landscape          of          life          will          be




           Diversified            by            one            less            degree





             For              eternity.






               And                the                brightness                of                life                --







                 That                  was                  me.








                   Will                    be                    gone                    in                    the                    interest                    of









                     Greed.
